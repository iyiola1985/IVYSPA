About Ivy Spa Studio

Meet Our Founder

My name is ADENIYI BISOLA, and I am the founder of ivyspa studio. I created this beauty studio to provide a clean, comfortable, and professional space where clients can enjoy quality beauty services in one place.

At ivyspa studio, we offer nail services, lash services, and hairstyling through skilled professionals who work within the studio. My focus is on maintaining high standards of hygiene, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

My goal is to create a welcoming environment where clients feel relaxed, confident, and well taken care of. At ivyspa studio, beauty is more than a service—it is an experience.
